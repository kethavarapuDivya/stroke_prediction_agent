About Brain Stroke

Brain stroke is a serious medical condition that occurs when blood flow to the brain is interrupted. Early detection can save lives. Our AI model helps estimate stroke risk based on patient health data.

⚠ Symptoms

  • Sudden weakness
  • Difficulty speaking
  • Blurred vision
  • Severe headache
  • Loss of balance

🛡 Prevention

  • Exercise Daily
  • Healthy Diet
  • Control Blood Pressure
  • Quit Smoking
  • Regular Health Checkups
